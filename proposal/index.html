Why this program, why now

REMSA already operates a nurse-triage layer. This proposal is not a replacement. It is a complementary clinician escalation and community-routing layer that aligns the 21,607 Alpha and Omega callers per year with the right care: clinician-on-camera, Omega-ambulance, rideshare to clinic, mental-health crisis line, poison control, or law-enforcement non-emergency. The result is reduced bad-debt exposure on uninsured low-acuity transports, freed truck-hours for true emergencies, and a measurable community-health story for the board.
